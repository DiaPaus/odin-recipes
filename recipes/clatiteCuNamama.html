Clatite cu banana

Description

Best banana pancakes that my youngest kid likes

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 whole egg
  • 1 galbenus
  • cinnamon
  • 3 linguri de faina de orice fel, quinoa, naut, alba, integrala, etc

Steps

  1. Mash the banana with a pinch of salt
  2. Add cinnamon
  3. Add the egg and galbenus and mix
  4. Add faina amestecand serios si un pinch e bicarbonat
  5. Add coconut oil in a pancake tigaie la foc mic
  7. Cand s-a incalzit tigaia adaugati compozitia cu lingura
